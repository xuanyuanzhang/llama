Something about me

My name is Xuanyuan. I usually go by Bill. I am currently a junior student at Columbia University learning computer science.

You should have already seen me before entering this page. Yes! I am the only one on the homepage who avoid to become a llama.

I describe myself as someone who is good at everything except studying. From playing music to playing poker, fine arts appreciation to calligraphy and martial arts, let alone those related to computer such as doing photoshop and making digital music. I am usually the one who laugh the least, because I can make people with me to never stop laughing.

Front-end is more like an art than technology. It leaves plenty of space for my imagination to explode. By learning from other people's projects, what I get are not merely technical skills but some new aspects of aesthetic judgment, which is something I have been passionate about throughout my first twenty years of life. I will certainly go deeper in the field of computer science, but making website as a fundamental task will still be part of my interest.

Something technical about this project

I wrote these in Notepad using Bootstrap, a super useful front-end framework which combines HTML, CSS and js together. I used to play around with eclipse by making dynamic web projects, such as a log-in page and a forum, and I strongly feel that coding for front-end is such a pain. Bootstrap is such a wonderful tool I later discovered that can make web making a really enjoyable thing. Also, Bootstrap offers an open source community that brings me opportunities to have a look on other people's projects, which is a better way to improve than merely coding myself.
